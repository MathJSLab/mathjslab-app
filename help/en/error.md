- `error (TEMPLATE, ...)`
- `error (ID, TEMPLATE, ...)`

Display an error message and stop execution.

The message may be formatted with additional arguments. An optional identifier
may be supplied to classify the error.

See also: `warning`, `lasterror`, `rethrow`.
